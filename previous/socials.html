Fall Ball

We hold our fall ball every year around late October or early November. It is typically held the night of our ballroom competition, which puts it on November 13th for 2010. The fall ball can be expected to start around 8 PM, and will run past midnight. There will be a lesson for beginners, in addition to a ballroom showcase with guest performances by other Princeton groups.

Clover Ball

The Clover Ball is typically scheduled around March or April, and starts with a beginner lesson, followed by a showcase by the Princeton Ballroom Formation Team, before the floor is opened for general dancing.
