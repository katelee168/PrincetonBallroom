Lessons


Beginner

Beginner lessons are targeted towards people who have never danced before. These lessons start slowly with basic figures, with plenty of review between weeks. A veteran couple on the team will typically be leading these lessons, and the lessons are usually structured in blocks e.g. three weeks of waltz, three weeks of rumba, etc.

Intermediate

Intermediate lessons are typically taught by a veteran club member or Joelle Escoffrey. These lessons include more difficult figures and also go over competition technique in far more depth than beginner lessons.

Advanced

Advanced lessons are either taught by Joelle Escoffrey or a guest instructor. These lessons focus almost exclusively on competition technique and are geared towards those who have been dancing at least a semester intensively at the competition level.
