Fall Ball

We hold our fall ball every year around late October or early November. Clover Ball

The last Clover Ball was held on Saturday, April 7th, from 8pm to 12pm in Dillon Gym.
Featuring: Werner Figar, 3 time Austrian Champion and competitor in Austria's Dancing with the Stars, accompanying performances by Princeton Ballroom's formation team and solo couples.
