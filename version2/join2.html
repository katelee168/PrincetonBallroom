What do I need to do?

All you need to do is show up at one of our practices and introduce yourself to the team. There are no try-outs, but you should contact one of the team members and ask to be put on the ballroom e-mail list.

Are there any required lessons?

Officially, lessons are not required in any form, nor level-restricted. However, it is highly recommended that you attend all the lessons you are interested in, so you can figure out who would be an appropriate partner for you. In addition, if you attend lessons that are clearly beyond your skill level, the instructors will still gear the lesson towards the more advanced members of the team.

What do I need to pay?

Dues are $20 for the first semester. Afterwards, dues will depend on which lessons you elect to take. All dues go towards paying for our instructors.
