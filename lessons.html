Lessons


Beginner

Beginner lessons are targeted towards people who are new to ballroom dancing. These lessons start slowly with basic figures, with plenty of review between weeks. A veteran couple on the team will typically be leading these lessons, and the lessons are usually structured in blocks e.g. three weeks of waltz, three weeks of rumba, etc.

Intermediate

Intermediate lessons are intended for people who have had at least a semester of experience in partnered dancing. These lessons will cover more difficult figures and go over competition technique in far more depth than beginner lessons.

Advanced

Intended for dancers who have attained silver level or above in competitions, these lessons will focus on refining competitive technique, and are usually supplemented by private instuction by one of our professional coaches.
